# Saral — Where Reading Is Accessible

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue.svg)]()

> Supportive tools for people with reading difficulties — customizable text, color-coding, and read-aloud support in English & Nepali.

---

## 📖 Overview

**Saral** is an accessibility tool designed to make reading simpler, calmer, and more inclusive. It helps users with reading difficulties (including dyslexia, visual stress, or other neurodiversity) by offering reading support features like:

- Text-to-speech (read aloud), with word-by-word highlighting  
- Highlighting of confusing letter pairs (e.g. **b / d**, **p / q**, **6 / 9**)  
- Adjustable typography: letter spacing, line height, paragraph gaps  
- Clean, minimal interface that doesn’t distract from reading  
- Support for **English** and **Nepali**  
- Built for “every mind” — to adapt reading to the user, not the other way around  

---

## 🧩 Features

- **Listen while you read** — Saral reads text aloud and highlights each word as it goes  
- **Color-code confusing letters** — Optionally emphasize letter pairs that are often misread  
- **Customizable spacing & layout** — Adjust letter spacing, line height, paragraph gap to suit your reading style  
- **Multilingual support** — Works with both English and Nepali text  
- **Lightweight & minimal UI** — Designed to stay out of your way and help you focus  
- **Testimonials & feedback** — Real users share how Saral helps them connect with text
